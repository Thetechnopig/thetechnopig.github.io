Activities

Combat

The regiment fights on land, in the depths or on the oceans: tank battles, battleship duels, bayonet fights, heroic grenade charges, submarine infiltrations, artillery bombardments, and discreet commando operations. All of these tactics are paramount to winning in Foxhole. Join our regiment to experience intense combat.

Logistics

By train, truck, or ship, the regiment harvests, produces and delivers tens of thousands of resources every day to the front for a Colonial victory. Logistics is the backbone of our regiment, ensuring uninterrupted support to our forces and allies on the field.

Construction

The Colonial shield: fortresses built by the regiment are of unanimously recognized quality. Each war, our sappers build on the front lines, and our architects create strongholds to stop the colonial onslaught. Join our clan to participate in the construction of impregnable and strategic defenses.

Industry

Renowned for its infrastructure, the regiment establishes an industrial and railway network every war to produce all that is needed for combat: oil, concrete, super tanks, heavy railway guns, submarines, battleships, etc. Our regiment is known for its extremely efficient facilites and its ability to support the war effort with large scale logistics.

Community

Mainly focused on Foxhole, VOID has a secret HQ for meetups over a beer and a gaming Discord to relax between military campaigns. Our regiment is a welcoming and tight-knit community where every member finds their place and role.
