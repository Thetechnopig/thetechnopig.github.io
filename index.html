11. If you are downed in a shootout/fight, wait until EMS/PD arrives to be transported to the hospital or declared dead. If EMS/PD is not ON DUTY, you are allowed to be transported to the HOSPITAL, but the driver CAN NOT CARRY and must place in seat. (BANNABLE OFFENSE)

11.1 NO MOTORCYCLE CARRYING ALLOWED

13.1 DON’T RUN from PD/EMS must comply until cleared,arrested,hospitalized. You NEED to interact for at least 15 seconds, pull over if you're being stopped, like in real life)
